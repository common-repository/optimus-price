Optimus Price automates pricing using Artificial Intelligence. It maximizes profit for your existing products.

== Description ==

Optimus Price gives machine learning superpowers to pricing managers. You will increase your profits through dynamic pricing and demand prediction and lower your operative costs through full catalog automation.

Optimus Price provides exact pricing recommendations through dynamic pricing experiments that find the optimal demand curve for every single product.

With Optimus Price, pricing managers can learn which are the prices that maximize profits for every product, discover hidden opportunities in the long tail, and manage large catalogs automatically.

Installation is straightforward and the system requires no maintenance. Try out Optimus Price for free today!

== Installation ==

Download the archive to your WooCommerce installation, or add the plugin directly from the plugins repository.

The plugin will guide you with the setup.
